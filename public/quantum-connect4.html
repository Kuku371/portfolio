Quantum Connect 4
A quantum twist on the classic Connect 4 game where coins exist in superposition until measured.

Features
• Superposition coins with quantum probabilities
• Entanglement between quantum states
• Quantum gates (Pauli-X and Hadamard)

Originally built in Python with Tkinter & Turtle; ported to the web.
